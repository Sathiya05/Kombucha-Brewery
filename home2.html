It all started with a mason jar, a SCOBY named Gerald, and a stubborn belief that healthy drinks shouldn't taste like punishment. In 2017, FizzyCulture was born in a Portland garage kitchen.

Today we brew in a 5,000 sq ft taproom, but the obsession hasn't changed: every batch is hand-crafted, wild-fermented, and bursting with the kind of flavour that makes you close your eyes on the first sip.
